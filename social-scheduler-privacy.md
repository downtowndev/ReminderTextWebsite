# Privacy Policy for Social Scheduler (Instagram, Facebook, Threads)

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Andrew Sverdrup ("we", "us", "our") processes personal data when you use our social scheduler service that allows users to connect Instagram, Facebook, and Threads accounts and schedule posts.

If you have questions, contact: `remindertextappteam@gmail.com`

## 1. Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to users of our social scheduler app and related services that connect to Meta platforms (Instagram, Facebook, Threads).

## 2. Data Controller

Controller/Operator: **Andrew Sverdrup**  
Contact email: `remindertextappteam@gmail.com`

## 3. Data We Process

Depending on your use of the service, we process:

- Connected account identifiers (for example: profile ID, page ID, Instagram business account ID, Threads account ID).
- OAuth tokens and token metadata needed to maintain authorized connections.
- Scheduling metadata (scheduled time, destination account, posting options, workflow state).
- Publish status and error logs (success/failure status, API response metadata, timestamp, retry state).
- Basic account and support data you provide (such as contact email and support messages).
- Security and operational logs (for abuse prevention, troubleshooting, and service reliability).

We do not sell or license Platform Data.  
We do not process Platform Data to build or augment user profiles for unauthorized purposes.

## 4. How and Why We Process Data

We process data to:

- Connect your Instagram, Facebook, and Threads accounts.
- Schedule and publish content at your direction.
- Authenticate sessions and keep integrations active.
- Detect, prevent, and investigate misuse, fraud, and security incidents.
- Provide customer support and respond to legal obligations.

We process Platform Data only as described in this policy and as permitted by applicable Meta terms, developer policies, and applicable law.

## 5. Legal Bases (Global Baseline)

Where applicable, we rely on:

- Contractual necessity (to provide requested scheduling and publishing functionality).
- Legitimate interests (service security, abuse prevention, reliability, and support).
- Consent (where required by law or platform requirements).
- Legal obligation (when retention or disclosure is legally required).

## 6. Sharing and Processors

We may share data with service providers acting on our behalf (for example, hosting, cloud infrastructure, monitoring, and support tooling) under contractual restrictions.

We do not sell personal information.  
We do not share Platform Data with third parties except as needed to provide the service, comply with law, or as directed by you.

## 7. Retention

We retain data only for as long as needed for the purposes above, subject to legal requirements:

- OAuth tokens and account connection data: while your account is connected, and generally up to 30 days after disconnection or verified deletion request.
- Scheduling metadata and publish status/error logs: while needed for scheduling history, support, and reliability, and generally up to 12 months after creation unless earlier deletion is requested.
- Security and operational logs: generally up to 90 days, unless longer retention is required for security investigations or legal compliance.
- Support communications: generally up to 24 months from last support interaction.

Aggregated or de-identified data may be retained longer where it can no longer be linked to you.

## 8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than where you live. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers, such as contractual protections and access controls.

## 9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal data, including access controls, encryption in transit, and least-privilege operational practices. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Request access to your personal data.
- Request correction of inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your data.
- Object to or restrict certain processing.

You can also revoke app access through your Meta account settings.

## 11. How To Request Deletion

All users who can access our app can request deletion.

- Use our deletion instructions page:  
`https://downtowndev.github.io/ReminderTextWebsite/social-scheduler-data-deletion`
- Or email: `remindertextappteam@gmail.com`

For verified requests, we aim to complete deletion within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law.

## 12. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under California law to:

- Know what categories of personal information we collect, use, disclose, and retain.
- Request deletion of personal information (subject to legal exceptions).
- Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
- Exercise rights without discrimination.

To submit a California privacy request, email `remindertextappteam@gmail.com` with subject line `California Privacy Request`.

## 13. Minors

Our service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
