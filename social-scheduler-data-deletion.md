# Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of data associated with the social scheduler app that connects Instagram, Facebook, and Threads accounts.

This page is intended to be used as the **User Data Deletion** instructions URL in the Meta App Dashboard.

## 1. Revoke app access from Meta (recommended first step)

You can remove app access from your Meta account at any time:

- Facebook: Settings and privacy -> Settings -> Apps and Websites -> Remove this app.
- Instagram/Threads: Use Meta account center settings to remove connected app access.

Revoking access stops new data collection through Meta APIs, but does not by itself delete historical data already stored by us.

## 2. Request deletion from us

Email `remindertextappteam@gmail.com` with subject line:

`Meta Data Deletion Request`

Please include:

- The email address associated with your account (if available).
- Your connected Meta account handle or profile URL.
- Any identifier that helps us locate your data (for example, Page ID, Instagram Business Account ID, or App-Scoped User ID, if known).

## 3. What we delete

For verified requests, we delete or de-identify data we control that is associated with your request, including:

- OAuth tokens and related connection credentials.
- Connected account/profile/page identifiers.
- Scheduled post metadata and publish status/error records linked to your account.

We may retain limited records when required by law, for security incident investigation, or to enforce legal rights.

## 4. Timing and confirmation

- We confirm receipt of your request within 5 business days.
- We complete verified deletion requests within 30 days.
- If a request is unusually complex, we will notify you of any lawful extension.

After processing, we will send confirmation by email.
